2. District-Level Demographic Profile & Displacement

Mudug faces significant pressure from internal displacement, with over 70,000 IDPs recorded in the region. This, combined with a high proportion of children (≤18), creates a critical need for educational stability.

4. Planning Recommendations

  1. Galkayo Focus: Gaalkacyo remains the highest priority area, containing the majority of both high and medium-risk schools.
  2. Extended Buffer: The inclusion of an 11-25km buffer identifies 3 additional schools (Midnimo, Qorqora, Docol) that may be impacted by population displacement.
  3. Contingency Hubs: The 34 low-risk schools should be evaluated for their capacity to absorb additional students during escalations.
