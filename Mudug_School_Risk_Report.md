# Mudug School Risk Proximity Report (2023-2024)
## Prepared for: EBI Education Continuity Project

### 1. Executive Summary
This report analyzes the proximity of educational facilities in the Mudug region to recent conflict events (2023-2024). The goal is to identify schools at high risk of disruption or those likely to serve as hubs for displaced populations.

*   **Total Schools Assessed:** 47
*   **High Risk (≤ 5km from conflict):** 10
*   **Medium Risk (5km - 15km from conflict):** 0
*   **Low Risk (> 15km from conflict):** 37

### 4. Planning Recommendations
1.  **Galkayo Hotspot:** With the highest concentration of high-risk schools, Galkayo should be the primary focus for safety-focused teacher training and psychological support.
2.  **Infrastructure Hardening:** Schools listed as 0.0km from conflict should be assessed for structural damage or occupation by armed actors.
3.  **Contingency Hubs:** Low-risk schools in Gaalkacyo and Hobyo should be evaluated for their capacity to absorb additional students fleeing high-risk zones.
