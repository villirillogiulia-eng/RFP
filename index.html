Mudug School Risk Proximity Report (2023-2024)

Prepared for: EBI Education Continuity Project

1. Executive Summary

This report analyzes the proximity of educational facilities in the Mudug region to recent conflict events (2023-2024). High-risk schools are those located within 10km of a conflict point, while medium-risk schools are between 10km and 25km. The region is characterized by an extremely young demographic profile, with approximately 57% of the population under age 15.

  • Total Population (Est. 2022): 864,728
  • Estimated Children (Under 15): 492,895
  • Total Schools Assessed: 47
  • High Risk (≤ 10km from conflict): 10
  • Medium Risk (10km - 25km from conflict): 3
  • Low Risk (> 25km from conflict): 34

4. Planning Recommendations

  1. Galkayo Focus: Gaalkacyo remains the highest priority area, containing the majority of both high and medium-risk schools.
  2. Extended Buffer: The inclusion of an 11-25km buffer identifies 3 additional schools (Midnimo, Qorqora, Docol) that may be impacted by population displacement or road blockages.
  3. Contingency Hubs: The 34 low-risk schools should be evaluated for their capacity to absorb additional students during escalations.
